CheckList before CI

Step 1: Check Warning:

step:

1.   Find checkWarning.bat in the root folder of your drive, like w:/

2.   Double click this bat

3.   Script will rebuild cypher/bandit to find out if new warning have been introduced or not,about 10 minutes is needed

Step 2: Check Klocwork:

step:

1.   Find checkKlocwork.bat in the root folder of your drive, like w:/

2.   Double click this bat

3.   Script will compare with the klocwork data in server, the consume time decided by the FCL you make
